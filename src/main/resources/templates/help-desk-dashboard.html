Welcome, Help Desk Officer!

This is the main dashboard for managing and assigning all incoming support tickets. Coordinate support efforts and ensure timely responses to student inquiries.

Help Desk Officer Responsibilities

As a Help Desk Officer, you are responsible for:

  • Ticket Assignment: Assign incoming tickets to appropriate support staff
  • Priority Management: Identify and prioritize urgent support requests
  • Coordination: Ensure smooth workflow between different support teams
  • Quality Control: Monitor response times and support quality
